COURSE OFFERED AT DR.K.B. MAPARI INSTITUTE OF NURSING,(ANM), LONAR

Auxiliary nurse midwife (A.N.M)

The Dr.K.B.Mapari Institute of Nursing,(ANM),Lonar is attached to the Polyclinic, offering Two Years Diploma Course in Auxiliary nurse midwife (A.N.M) following the Maharashtra Nursing Council Syllabus & Director, Medical Education & Research.

Course Details

1) Name of the Course :- Auxiliary nurse midwife (A.N.M)

2) Duration :- 2 Years

3) Status :- Unaided

4) Authorities by :- Medical Education & Drugs Dept. Govt. of Maharashtra. (DMER)

5) Approved Accorded by :- Maharashtra Nursing Council (MNC)

6) Affiliation :- INDIAN NURSING COUNCIL (INC)

MAHARASHTRA STATE BOARD OF NURSING AND PARAMEDICAL EDUCATION (MSBNPE)

Eligibility Criteria

A.N.M

1) The minimum age for admission shall be 17 years on or before 31st December of the year in which admission is sought.

2) The maximum age for admission shall be 35 years.

3) The minimum educational requirements shall be 10 + 2 in Arts (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Biotechnology, Economics, Political Science, History, Geography, Business Studies, Accountancy, Home Science, Sociology, Psychology, and Philosophy) and English Core/English Elective or Science or Health care Science - Vocational stream ONLY passing out from recognized Board.

4) Student shall be medically fit.

5) Students qualified in 10+2 Arts or Science examination conducted by National Institute of Open School.

6) Student shall be admitted once in a year.
