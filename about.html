ABOUT US

Dr. K. B. Mapari Institute of Nursing,(ANM),Lonar

Dr. K. B. Mapari Institute of Nursing,(ANM),Lonar was established in 2011, in order to fulfill the acute shortage of trained nursing personnel. It is one of the best nursing colleges in lonar(Buldhana). Dr. K. B. Mapari Institute of Nursing,(ANM),Lonar offers a Two Years' Diploma Course in Auxiliary nurse midwife (A.N.M). The Course follows Indian Nursing Council's (I.N.C.) & Maharashtra Nursing Council's (M.N.C.) syllabus, alongwith Director Medical Education & Research (DMER) guidelines.The Institute is being managed by a well qualified faculties.It is well known for its high standards of Nursing Education and excellence in academic achievement.
